About Me

I'm Peter Philavanh

Background

A little background about myself, I was born in Thailand and came to the states when I was just a baby. My family landed here in Portland and have been here ever since. I would consider myself a Portland native. I grew up in the Beaverton area during the era of 56K dialup internet and have seen the growth and development of all things related to the internet. I went to college at Portland State and hold a Bachelor's degree in Biology. I currently am employed at Oregon Health Science University in the Neurosciences Med surge unit. I started in 2020 right before the pandemic. Working through the pandemic made me realize that healthcare was not something I wanted to continue pursuing a career in.


This lead to my choice to enroll into the coding bootcamp at Epicodus. I chose to do so because I believe the tech industry is here to stay and has had steady growth with continuation of growth many years into the future. I am a very curious person with how things work. Whether it be car engines, bikes, software, or webpages; I like knowing how things function. Learning how to program has fullfilled my curiousity and at the same time, it has challenged me.

Current Interests and Hobbies

I am that person who has too many hobbies and interests for the amount of available time I currently have. Some major current interests are DJing or mixing, cooking, woodworking, fishing, hiking, overlanding (offroading with a destination), building and modifying cars. During the winter I am an avid snowboarder aswell. There are too many things that always pique my interest and I tend to dabble into it for a while.
